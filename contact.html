Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for studying abroad?

The requirements for studying abroad vary depending on the country, university, and course you are interested in. Generally, you will need to have a good academic record, be proficient in English, and have sufficient funds to cover your tuition fees and living expenses.

How much does it cost to study abroad?

The cost of studying abroad varies depending on the country, university, and course you are interested in. Generally, you can expect to pay between $10,000 and $50,000 per year for tuition fees and living expenses.

Can I work while I am studying abroad?

Most countries allow international students to work part-time while they are studying. However, there may be restrictions on the number of hours you can work and the type of work you can do.

How long does the visa application process take?

The visa application process typically takes 4-12 weeks depending on the country and type of visa. We will keep you updated throughout the process and ensure all documents are submitted correctly to avoid delays.

What is the success rate of visa applications?

Our success rate is very high due to our thorough preparation and attention to detail. We ensure all applications are complete, accurate, and meet all requirements before submission.

Do you provide assistance with scholarship applications?

Yes, we provide guidance on available scholarships and help you prepare strong scholarship applications to increase your chances of receiving financial aid.

What makes UPSAIC different from other consultancies?

UPSAIC is led by a dual national consultant with extensive experience in both Pakistan and the UK. We provide honest, credible, and personalized guidance specifically tailored to students from the Malakand Division region, ensuring affordable and accessible services.
