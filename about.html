About UPSAIC

Our region (Malakand Division) is the largest region of Khyber Pukhtunkhwa (KP) province and encompasses 7 districts with huge qualified leads who can be converted into customers easily. The region is comprised of four public sector universities and multiple degree colleges (public and private sector) including technical and nursing colleges. We promise to deliver good value for money and are professionally confident that our guidance and facilitation will enable the intenders to realise their dream in an affordable manner.

Our Mission

To provide person-specific and person-centred facilitation to the youth and qualified segment of our forgotten region, enabling them to make their choices and transform their future together through honest, credible and authentic counselling.

Our Motto

“Let's Plan Your Career Together” is more than a line — it is how we co-create every application, interview and relocation plan with our students and their families.

The Lead Consultant

At UPSAIC, the Lead Consultant is a dual national (Pakistani and British), born and brought up in this particular region (Swat). He has served as Auditor in Government Treasury, Area Manager in a financial institution (SME Bank of Pakistan) and a Project Manager in this region for more than 15 years. In addition, he worked as a social worker in the region and has established a viable and sustainable working relationship within the region.

In 2004, he migrated to the United Kingdom (UK) under the Highly Skilled Migration Program (HSMP) and worked in multiple sectors to establish his family in the United Kingdom. Thereafter, he completed his Masters in International Development Management from the University of Bradford, UK. Alongside, he studied UK-based Immigration Rules, gained practical experience in visa appeals, administrative reviews and Pre-Action Protocols of the Home Office. He also critically analyse the UK education system and admission processes in different universities of the UK.

In 2015, he has changed his profession on compassionate grounds, obtained BSc (Hons) in Adult General Nursing and started working as a healthcare professional in the NHS, UK. Since then, he is working in the NHS, UK.
