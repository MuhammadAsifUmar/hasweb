Affordable MBBS & Dentistry in Europe

The UPSAIC pathway (page 10–12 of our PDF) highlights flexible routes for undergraduate and graduate-entry Medicine and Dentistry candidates across Armenia, Georgia, Serbia, Romania and Bulgaria.

Undergraduate Entry

  • MBBS lasts 6 years and Dentistry lasts 5 years (pre-clinical + clinical modules).
  • World-class lectures, seminars and modern laboratories with top-ranked faculty.
  • English proficiency certificate accepted; functional skills courses available.
  • Scholarships and instalment plans available in many universities.
  • Entry exam requirements vary (some waive tests; others require Chemistry/Biology).

Graduate Entry (4-year)

  • Designed for BSc Chemistry/Biology graduates or registered nurses.
  • Direct entry to 2nd or 3rd year with no entry exam in select universities.
  • Blended delivery possible (interactive live-streamed classes and question banks).
  • Clinical placements in local hospitals and, subject to availability, UK/Canada/USA.
  • Perfect for accelerating progression while retaining English-medium teaching.

Generic Admissions & Scholarship Tips

Generic Requirements

  • Application form & valid passport
  • Personal statement & Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Academic certificates, transcripts & CV
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) and references
  • Work experience / internships where applicable
  • CAS / offer letter, course fee & visa fee
  • Bank statements, maintenance funds & dependant finances
  • Medical fitness, police clearance & research proposals (for PhD)

Letter for Scholarship

Use the following structure to communicate merit + need:

  • Header & greeting tailored to the awarding body
  • Brief introduction with academic background
  • Clear Statement of Purpose with measurable goals
  • Summary of academic achievements & community impact
  • Motivation and long-term objectives
  • Transparent financial need statement
  • Positive closing with commitment to represent the sponsor

How to Apply

To apply for our programs, please follow these steps:

  1. Choose a course and university that you are interested in.
  2. Contact us to schedule a free consultation.
  3. Submit your application and required documents.
  4. We will guide you through the rest of the process.

Required Documents:

  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • English language proficiency test results (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Letters of Recommendation (LOR)
  • Passport copy
  • Financial documents
